About

"I, an atom in the Universe, a Universe of atoms." - Richard P. Feynman

I am currently a doctoral researcher in Solar physics within the School of Mathematics and Statistics at The University of Sheffield, funded by the Science and Technology Facilities Council.

Solar physics is a branch of astrophysics that focuses on our parent star, the Sun - an interdisciplinary field of science with the utmost importance to our planet. As part of the Plasma Dynamics Group, my main research efforts are focused towards understanding how the ubiqituous magnetohydrodynamic waves can heat the Sun's atmosphere.
